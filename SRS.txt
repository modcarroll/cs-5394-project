Non Functional Requirements


1. Reliability:
The application shall be able to turn off or turn on connected lights and tvs 95% of the time.
The application shall be able to lock or unlock connected doors 95% of the time.
The application shall be able to turn up or turn down connect thermostats and speakers 95 % of the time.

2. Robustness:
In the case of error occurance proper error messages will be displayed on the application screen

3. Maintainability
Application updates shall take no longer than 30 minutes to download
No more than two updates per month will be pushed to the application

4. Security
The application shall store user interactions in a database
